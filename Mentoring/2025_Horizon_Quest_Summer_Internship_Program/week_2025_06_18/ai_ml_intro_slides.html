🤔 What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines

Think about it: When you recognize a friend's face in a crowd, your brain processes visual information instantly. AI tries to replicate this kind of intelligence!

Examples you use daily:

  • Spotify recommending songs you might like
  • Google Translate converting languages
  • Siri or Alexa understanding your voice
  • Netflix suggesting movies
  • Instagram's photo filters
